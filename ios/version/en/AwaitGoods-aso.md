## App Description

AwaitGoods is a lightweight app for wish lists, shopping lists, and savings progress.

When you find something you want, you do not have to buy it immediately. Put it into AwaitGoods first, add the price, saved amount, category, link, and notes, then use progress, status, and priority to decide whether it is still worth moving toward.

Good for:

- Tracking products, clothes, home items, gifts, books, or media you want
- Managing a personal wishlist or shopping list
- Seeing saved amount and remaining amount before buying
- Reducing impulse purchases and duplicate purchases
- Keeping a local-first, account-free, quiet list

Key features:

- Quick add with price, saved amount, category, and link
- Home list with status, priority, and savings progress
- Detail view for adding savings or filling the target
- Statuses for wanted, owned, and let go
- Search from the top-right icon, filters, manual drag sorting, and batch organizing
- Home screen widgets for wishlist summaries and savings progress
- JSON export and import
- Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, English, Japanese, French, German, Italian, and Korean
- Light, dark, and system appearance modes
- Refined theme palettes for different moods

AwaitGoods is not a store and not a complex budgeting app. It is a quiet wishlist: save the idea, let the decision breathe, and bring home only what still feels worth owning.

## App Review Notes

AwaitGoods is a local-first wishlist and shopping list utility. It has no account system, no paid features, and no third-party login.

Reviewers can test the main flow from the home screen: tap the floating add button, create a wish, open search from the top-right icon, filter and sort, open details, add saved amount, switch status, export JSON, view widgets, change language, and switch theme.

App data is stored locally by default. Product links are entered by the user and opened through the system browser.
