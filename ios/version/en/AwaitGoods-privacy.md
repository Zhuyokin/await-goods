# AwaitGoods Privacy Policy (English)

Effective date: 2026-05-02

If you use AwaitGoods, you agree to the data handling described in this privacy policy.

AwaitGoods is a local-first wishlist and shopping list app. We keep data processing minimal and only handle information needed for the app’s core features.

## 1. Who We Are

- Product name: AwaitGoods
- Developer website: https://mrqk.com
- Email: yokinzhu@gmail.com
- WeChat: Zhuyokin

## 2. Information We Process

The following information may be stored locally on your device:

- Wish items you create, such as name, price, saved amount, link, category, notes, status, priority, and mark color
- Sorting information, creation time, and update time
- Preferences such as appearance mode, theme palette, language, and widget display
- JSON backup files you choose to export or import

## 3. How We Use This Information

This information is used only to:

- Display and manage your wishlist and shopping list
- Calculate and show savings progress
- Show waiting item summaries in widgets
- Support search, filters, sorting, export, and import
- Save language and visual preferences

## 4. What We Do Not Do

In the current version, AwaitGoods does not:

- Require account registration
- Upload your wishlist data to developer servers
- Sell your personal information
- Use advertising tracking SDKs to profile you or track you across apps
- Share your data without an action you choose to take

## 5. Permissions

The core features in the current version do not require account registration or extra permissions for advertising, marketing, or promotion. If future versions add optional reminders, those notifications will also be triggered locally on your device.

## 6. Widgets

If you use home screen widgets, the app writes limited item summary data to the system-approved App Group container so the widget can read it. This data remains on your device and is not uploaded to external servers.

## 7. Export, Import, and Sharing

When you use export, the app creates a JSON file on your device. You decide where to save, send, or back up that file. Import reads only the JSON file you choose.

## 8. External Links

You may save product links in AwaitGoods. Opening a link may launch a third-party website or app. Those services are controlled by third parties and have their own privacy practices.

## 9. When You Contact Us

If you contact support by email or WeChat, we receive the information you choose to provide, such as your contact details, issue description, screenshots, or device information. We use it only to respond, handle feedback, and improve the product.

## 10. Data Storage and Deletion

Your AwaitGoods data is stored locally on your device by default.

You can delete data by:

- Clearing all data in app settings
- Deleting individual wish items
- Uninstalling the app from your device
- Deleting exported backup files yourself

## 11. Children’s Privacy

AwaitGoods is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect children’s personal information.

## 12. Policy Updates

If future versions add accounts, cloud sync, analytics, or third-party SDKs, we will update this privacy policy and explain the changes in a reasonable way.
