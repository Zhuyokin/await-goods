## App Description

AwaitGoods is a local-first wishlist and shopping decision app for people who want to save ideas before spending. Add an item, record its price and saved amount, and decide more calmly with progress and status.

Core experience:

- Save name, price, saved amount, category, link, notes, priority, and status
- Use search, filters, manual sorting, batch organizing, and savings progress on the home list
- Add savings or fill the target from the detail screen
- Show wishlist summaries and progress in home screen widgets
- Import and export JSON for offline backup
- Switch between multiple theme styles, including fresh Japanese minimal, creamy minimal, and Apple-like minimal directions
- Support Simplified Chinese, Traditional Chinese, English, Japanese, Korean, German, Italian, and French
- Support light mode, dark mode, and system appearance
- Include onboarding, launch screen, support page, and privacy policy page

AwaitGoods is not a store and not a complex budgeting tool. It is a quiet wishlist that helps users slow down, keep track of intent, and bring home only what still feels worth owning.
